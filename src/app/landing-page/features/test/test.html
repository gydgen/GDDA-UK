About GDDA-UK

Professionalism with a Purpose.

flagOur Mission

To unite Ghanaian doctors and dentists in the UK into one strong association, providing support, mentorship, and a collective voice in their careers, while contributing meaningfully to the healthcare sector in Ghana.

visibilityOur Vision

To be a trusted "home away from home" for Ghanaian healthcare professionals in the UK, built on truth and equality, fostering unity, professional growth, and impactful contributions to both the UK and Ghana's health systems.
