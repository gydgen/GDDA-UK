Milestones of Excellence

2020 — 2021

COVID-19 Response & Humanitarian Aid

GDDA-UK made history as the first Ghanaian Diasporan society to donate directly to Ghana during the global crisis. We committed a total of £9,900 to support frontline workers and health centers across the nation.

  • May 2020: £7,500 Support Distributed to 3 major medical centres during the height of the first wave.
  • August 2021: £2,400 Relief Donated to 3 hospitals to bolster infrastructure and equipment reserves.
2017 — 2019

Restoring Dignity: The Obstetric Fistula Fund

We donated £3,000 to transform lives for women suffering from obstetric fistula. Beyond surgical support, we focused on sustainable medical infrastructure and clinical empowerment.
