Who Can Join?

Doctors

  • MBBS or equivalent
  • GMC registered or in training
  • Ghanaian heritage

Dentists

  • BDS or equivalent
  • GDC registered or in training
  • Ghanaian heritage

Medical and dental students of Ghanaian heritage studying in the UK are also welcome to join as student members.

Membership Benefits

Professional Networking

Connect with 500+ Ghanaian doctors and dentists across the UK at events, conferences, and through our members-only directory.

Career Development

Access mentorship, CPD opportunities, and career guidance from senior members with experience across NHS and international settings.

Community Outreach

Participate in medical missions to Ghana, donate to health initiatives, and make a tangible difference to communities back home.

Peer Support

Find community among professionals who understand your journey — from relocation challenges to navigating the NHS as an internationally educated doctor.
